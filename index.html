Pablo Lemos

Cosmologist

MY NAME IS Pablo Lemos.

I work as a postdoctoral Research Fellow in cosmology at University of Sussex.

My work focuses on extracting cosmological information from large cosmological data sets, and on combining different observables. I am also very interested in Machine Learning applications to astrophysics and cosmology.

I work as part of three collaborations: The Dark Energy Survey (DES), the Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument (DESI). and the Simons Observatory.
